About Me

Where data meets purpose.

I work at the intersection of Data Management and Programs Implementation. Over the years, I have supported public health and humanitarian initiatives across HIV, tuberculosis, and maternal and newborn health, helping teams strengthen systems and use information to guide action.

My experience spans monitoring, evaluation, and learning, performance management, and digital data systems. I have led and supported the design of reporting structures, dashboards, and data collection processes that improve accountability, coordination, and decision making.

Beyond technical delivery, I collaborate with diverse teams, facilitate learning sessions, and provide strategic guidance that builds confidence in data use. I am committed to growing capacity, strengthening institutions, and enabling evidence-informed decisions that translate into real world impact.
